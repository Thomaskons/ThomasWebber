About Thomas Webber

Professional Biography

Born in 1982 in Schützenbrunnen, Switzerland, Thomas Webber grew up in a family deeply rooted in banking. From his father and grandfather, he inherited both knowledge and values, developing early on a passion for finance, investments, and understanding how financial systems shape people's lives.

After completing his education, Thomas began his career at two of Europe's most respected institutions: Credit Suisse in Switzerland and ING Group in the Netherlands. Over more than seven years, he built strong expertise in wealth management, corporate finance, and international investments.

Yet, he became increasingly disillusioned with traditional banking, where client interests were too often overshadowed by institutional profit. Choosing integrity over corporate comfort, Thomas transitioned into independent financial advisory, committed to delivering transparent, client-first solutions.

He went on to strengthen his expertise with roles at UBS Wealth Management and Allianz Global Investors, gaining deeper knowledge in portfolio strategy, retirement planning, and alternative investments.

Since 2019, Thomas has been a senior advisor at H&W Invest, where he has found his true professional calling: empowering individuals and families to secure their financial futures. At H&W Invest, he combines decades of knowledge with a transparent, ethical, and client-centered approach.

Today, Thomas Webber is regarded as a trusted advisor across Europe — respected for his independence, professionalism, and unwavering commitment to protecting and growing his clients' wealth.
